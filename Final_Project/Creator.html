Name

Sahar Babi

Where Are You From?

I was born in Miami, Florida.

Grew up in Winston-Salem, North Carolina and Roanoke, Virginia.

Now I live in Brooklyn with my brother and mother.

Why Did You Move to NYC?

  • More Opportunities.
  • I have a passion for acting and living in New York City seemed like the best place to be for school and jobs.

What Was the Place Like Where You Moved From?

I moved from Roanoke, Virginia. It was okay, there wasn’t much going on. Although, there wasn’t a lot of city events or entertainment options, there were outdoor activities. There were The Blue Ridge Mountains to see and fun trails like Roaring Run. Also,the population was only 99,660 people, as compared to Brooklyn with approximately 2.6 million people.

How Was the Transition to New York City?

It was difficult at the beginning, but now 1 year and a half later, it’s not as hard. It took several months to get comfortable riding the subways without getting confused. Also, it took some time to get used to the influx of people, everywhere. What’s difficult now and then is my homesickness for the rural landscapes I’ve missed. The mountains, dense forests, sounds of crickets and cicadas, and just sitting around a campfire. The places I’ve found in Brooklyn are similar to these landscapes I miss, which clears the homesick feeling.
